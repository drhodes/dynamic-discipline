Combinational devices use the static discipline to mitigate noise on their terminals. Sequential devices use the dynamic discipline focuses on time. enters the circuit by using lenciency during ... .timing to the mix and can elude even the most careful thinkers. So beware!

The plan is to use feedback with a lenient mux to build a reliable D-latch.

Here are the steps outlined in the sequential logic section of the computation structures notes

To reliably latch 1

Apply 1 to D, holding G=1

After another TPD, Q’ & D both valid for TPD; will hold Q=1 independently of G

Set G=0, while Q’ & D hold Q=D

After TPD, 1 appears at Q=Q’

After another TPD, G=0 and Q’ are sufficient to hold Q=1 independently of D

- To reliably latch 0: • Apply 0 to D, holding G=1 • After another TPD, Q’ & D both valid for TPD; will hold Q=0 independently of G • Set G=0, while Q’ & D hold Q=D • After TPD, 0 appears at Q=Q’ • After another TPD, G=0 and Q’ are sufficient to hold Q=0 independently of D
